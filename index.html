About Me

I grew up in Nelson County, Virginia. I was always interested in logic, puzzles, and math. I spent a significant amount of time in school performing in musical groups such as concert band, jazz band, and marching band. I play the alto saxophone and have played it for 13 years. I performed as a member of my high school and college marching bands for a total of 9 years (5 during middle and high school and 4 during my undergrad studies). Performing in marching band has given me experience at accomplishing goals through teamwork, practice, and dedication.

I received my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Liberty University in May 2020. I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science from Old Dominion University with an anticipated graduation date of May 2022.
